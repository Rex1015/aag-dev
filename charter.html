Safety & Compliance

Safety is Our Top Priority

Every charter operator in our network is thoroughly vetted for safety, certification, and operational standards before we recommend them to our clients.

🔒 Our Safety Vetting Process

We only work with operators who meet the highest international safety standards. Our vetting process includes:

  • Valid Air Operator Certificate (AOC) verification
  • IOSA (IATA Operational Safety Audit) status check
  • IS-BAO (International Standard for Business Aircraft Operations) compliance
  • ARG/US or Wyvern safety rating verification
  • Insurance coverage verification
  • Incident and accident history review
  • Crew qualifications and experience check
  • Aircraft maintenance records review

📋 Regulatory Compliance

All charter operations arranged by Aero Assist Global comply fully with ICAO standards, local civil aviation authority regulations, and bilateral air service agreements.

🛡 Insurance Coverage

Every operator in our network carries comprehensive aviation liability insurance meeting or exceeding international minimum requirements.

📡 Flight Monitoring

We monitor all charter flights in real time from departure to arrival, keeping you informed of progress and any changes throughout the operation.
