Ground Handling

Aero Assist Global provides professional, safe, and efficient ground handling services at airports worldwide. From the moment your aircraft touches down to the moment it takes off again — our experienced teams are on the ground to support every aspect of your operation.

✈️ Ramp Services 🧳 Baggage Handling 👥 Passenger Services 📦 Cargo Handling 🔧 Aircraft Services 🕐 24/7 Available

Overview

Comprehensive Ground Support at Every Airport

Our ground handling division is built on a foundation of safety, efficiency, and professionalism. We work with airlines, private operators, cargo carriers, and government aviation entities to deliver seamless ground operations.

What We Handle

Our ground handling teams are trained to manage all aspects of aircraft turnaround and passenger flow, ensuring minimal delays and maximum safety compliance.

  • Aircraft marshalling and parking coordination
  • Pushback and towing services
  • Passenger check-in and boarding assistance
  • Baggage loading, unloading, and sorting
  • Cargo acceptance, build-up, and breakdown
  • Aircraft cleaning and cabin preparation
  • Catering uplift coordination
  • Ground power unit (GPU) connection
  • Air conditioning and pre-conditioned air (PCA)
  • Potable water and lavatory servicing
  • De-icing and anti-icing services
  • Ramp safety supervision and FOD checks

🛬 Ramp Services

Our ramp teams ensure safe and efficient aircraft movements on the apron. From marshalling to pushback, every movement is executed with precision and in full compliance with airport safety regulations.

👥 Passenger Services

We provide courteous and professional passenger handling — from check-in assistance and boarding gate management to VIP and special needs passenger support.

📦 Cargo & Mail Handling

Our cargo teams handle general freight, express cargo, dangerous goods (DG), live animals, and valuable cargo — all in accordance with IATA and local regulatory standards.

Our Capabilities

Full-Spectrum Ground Handling Capabilities

Every service we provide is backed by trained personnel, modern equipment, and strict adherence to international aviation safety standards.

🛬

Aircraft Marshalling

Certified marshallers guide aircraft safely to and from parking positions, ensuring precise positioning and full compliance with airport procedures.

🧳

Baggage Handling

Efficient baggage loading, unloading, and sorting with full tracking capability. We handle standard, oversized, fragile, and priority baggage.

👥

Passenger Assistance

From economy to VIP — our passenger service agents provide professional assistance at every touchpoint of the passenger journey on the ground.

📦

Cargo Operations

Full cargo handling including acceptance, build-up, breakdown, storage, and delivery. Certified for dangerous goods and special cargo categories.

❄️

De-icing Services

Aircraft de-icing and anti-icing treatments using approved fluids and procedures to ensure safe operations in cold weather conditions.

⚡

Ground Power & Air

GPU connection, pre-conditioned air (PCA), and nitrogen servicing to support aircraft systems during ground stops without using APU.

🚿

Aircraft Cleaning

Interior and exterior aircraft cleaning services including cabin turnaround cleaning, deep cleaning, and lavatory and water servicing.

🍽

Catering Coordination

Liaison with catering providers for meal uplift, galley loading, and special meal requests in accordance with airline specifications.

🔒

Ramp Safety & Security

Comprehensive ramp safety supervision, FOD (Foreign Object Debris) checks, and security screening coordination at all handled stations.
