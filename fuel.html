Fuel Types

Aviation Fuels We Supply

We supply all major types of aviation fuel used by commercial, private, cargo, and military aircraft operators worldwide.

⛽

Jet-A1

The most widely used aviation turbine fuel in the world. Suitable for all jet-powered commercial, business, and military aircraft. Meets DEF STAN 91-091 and ASTM D1655 international specifications.

🌡 Freeze Point: -47°C ✈️ Turbine Engines
⛽

Jet-A

Similar to Jet-A1 but with a slightly higher freeze point. Primarily used in the United States domestic market. Available at select airports through our US fuel network.

🌡 Freeze Point: -40°C 🇺🇸 US Market
🛩

AvGas 100LL

Aviation Gasoline 100 Low Lead — the standard fuel for piston-engine aircraft used in general aviation, flight training, and light aircraft operations worldwide.

🔵 Low Lead 🛩 Piston Engines
🌿

SAF — Sustainable Aviation Fuel

Produced from sustainable feedstocks including waste materials, agricultural residues, and non-fossil sources. SAF can reduce lifecycle carbon emissions by up to 80% compared to conventional jet fuel.

♻️ Sustainable 📉 Low Emissions
🪖

JP-8 / F-34

Military grade aviation turbine fuel used by military aircraft operators. We coordinate JP-8 and F-34 supply for military and government aviation operations at authorized locations.

🪖 Military Grade 🏛 Government Ops
🚁

Jet-B / Wide-Cut Fuel

A wide-cut aviation turbine fuel used in extremely cold weather environments and by certain helicopter and military operators requiring its specific performance characteristics.

❄️ Cold Weather 🚁 Helicopters

Quality & Safety

Uncompromising Fuel Quality Standards

Aviation fuel quality is critical to flight safety. Every fuel uplift we coordinate meets the strictest international quality and safety standards.

🔬 Quality Assurance

All fuel supplied through Aero Assist Global meets or exceeds the following international quality specifications and standards:

  • DEF STAN 91-091 (Jet-A1 specification)
  • ASTM D1655 (Aviation Turbine Fuels)
  • ASTM D910 (AvGas specification)
  • IATA Fuel Quality Requirements (IFQRP)
  • Joint Inspection Group (JIG) standards
  • API gravity and density verification
  • Water content and contamination checks
  • Fuel appearance and clarity inspection

📋 Documentation

Every fuel uplift is accompanied by complete documentation including fuel release certificates, density records, quantity confirmations, and supplier quality certificates.

🔍 Supplier Vetting

We only work with fuel suppliers and into-plane service providers who are JIG certified and comply with all applicable aviation fuel quality and safety standards.

⚠️ Contamination Prevention

Strict contamination prevention protocols are followed at every uplift including filter checks, bonding and grounding procedures, and dead man's handle safety systems at all fuelling points.

Sustainability

Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

Aero Assist Global is committed to supporting the aviation industry's transition to more sustainable operations. We actively promote and coordinate SAF supply for operators committed to reducing their carbon footprint.

🌿 What is SAF?

Sustainable Aviation Fuel is produced from sustainable feedstocks and can be used as a drop-in replacement for conventional jet fuel — requiring no modifications to aircraft or engines.

  • Up to 80% reduction in lifecycle CO2 emissions
  • 100% compatible with existing jet engines
  • Meets all international aviation fuel specifications
  • Produced from waste, agricultural, and non-fossil sources
  • Approved by ASTM International standards
  • Supports ICAO CORSIA carbon offsetting goals

♻️ SAF Feedstocks

SAF can be produced from a variety of sustainable feedstocks including municipal solid waste, agricultural residues, used cooking oil, forestry waste, and non-food energy crops.

📍 SAF Availability

SAF is currently available at a growing number of airports worldwide. Contact our fuel team to check SAF availability at your destination and arrange supply where possible.

📋 CORSIA Compliance

We provide full SAF documentation to support your CORSIA compliance reporting and sustainability commitments including chain of custody certificates.
