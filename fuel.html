Quality & Safety

Uncompromising Fuel Quality Standards

Aviation fuel quality is critical to flight safety. Every fuel uplift we coordinate meets the strictest international quality and safety standards.

🔬 Quality Assurance

All fuel supplied through Aero Assist Global meets or exceeds the following international quality specifications and standards:

  • DEF STAN 91-091 (Jet-A1 specification)
  • ASTM D1655 (Aviation Turbine Fuels)
  • ASTM D910 (AvGas specification)
  • IATA Fuel Quality Requirements (IFQRP)
  • Joint Inspection Group (JIG) standards
  • API gravity and density verification
  • Water content and contamination checks
  • Fuel appearance and clarity inspection

📋 Documentation

Every fuel uplift is accompanied by complete documentation including fuel release certificates, density records, quantity confirmations, and supplier quality certificates.

🔍 Supplier Vetting

We only work with fuel suppliers and into-plane service providers who are JIG certified and comply with all applicable aviation fuel quality and safety standards.

⚠️ Contamination Prevention

Strict contamination prevention protocols are followed at every uplift including filter checks, bonding and grounding procedures, and dead man's handle safety systems at all fuelling points.
