Hotel Services

Aero Assist Global provides seamless hotel accommodation solutions for flight crews, layover passengers, and VIP travelers worldwide. From budget-friendly crew hotels to five-star luxury suites — we handle every booking with precision, speed, and care. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Overview

Accommodation Solutions for Every Operation

Whether your crew needs a quick layover rest or your VIP passenger requires a luxury suite, Aero Assist Global coordinates hotel bookings at competitive rates with seamless transport integration worldwide.

What We Offer

Our hotel desk operates around the clock to ensure your crew and passengers are always accommodated comfortably and efficiently — no matter the time zone or destination.

  • 24/7 hotel reservation and coordination desk
  • Crew hotel bookings at negotiated special rates
  • VIP and executive suite arrangements
  • Passenger layover accommodation management
  • Airport proximity hotel selection
  • Group bookings for large crews and delegations
  • Hotel-to-airport transport coordination
  • Early check-in and late check-out arrangements
  • Meal and catering preferences coordination
  • Last-minute and emergency bookings handled

👨‍✈️ Crew Accommodation

We understand the unique requirements of flight crews — rest periods, proximity to airports, noise-free rooms, and reliable wake-up calls. Our crew hotel partners are carefully selected to meet airline and regulatory standards.

🌟 VIP & Executive Suites

For high-profile passengers, executives, and dignitaries, we arrange premium accommodations at the finest hotels worldwide — with full concierge support and privacy guaranteed.

🚨 Emergency Bookings

Flight diversions, technical delays, and unexpected layovers happen. Our team is ready 24/7 to arrange immediate hotel accommodation for any number of passengers or crew at short notice.
