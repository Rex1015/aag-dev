Permit Types

All Types of Aviation Permits Covered

No matter what type of flight you are operating, our permits team has the expertise and the contacts to get your clearances processed on time.

🌍

Overflight Permits

Authorization to fly through a country's airspace without landing. We handle single, multiple, and blanket overflight permits for all route types and aircraft categories.

🛬

Landing Permits

Required for any aircraft landing at a foreign airport. We process landing permits for scheduled, charter, private, cargo, and special mission flights at airports worldwide.

🤝

Diplomatic Clearances

State and government aircraft require diplomatic clearances processed through official channels. We handle these with the utmost discretion and professionalism.

🪖

Military Permits

Military overflight and landing authorizations require specialized handling through defense ministries and military aviation authorities. Our team has extensive experience in this area.

🌿

Humanitarian Permits

Relief and humanitarian flights often require expedited permit processing. We prioritize these requests and work around the clock to secure clearances as quickly as possible.

🕐

Slot Coordination

At slot-controlled airports, we coordinate arrival and departure slots with airport coordinators to ensure your flight operates at your preferred times without conflicts.

☢️

Dangerous Goods Permits

Transport of dangerous goods requires special approvals from civil aviation authorities. We handle DG permit applications in compliance with IATA DGR and ICAO Technical Instructions.

🔄

Cabotage & Traffic Rights

Applications for cabotage rights, traffic rights, and bilateral air service agreement (BASA) exemptions for operators requiring special route authorizations.

📋

NOTAM Filing

We coordinate NOTAM (Notice to Airmen) filings for special flight operations, airspace reservations, and other operational requirements with the relevant AIS authorities.

What We Need From You

Documents Typically Required

To process your permits efficiently, we typically require the following documents. Additional documents may be required depending on the country and type of operation.

✈️ Aircraft Documents

  • Certificate of Registration (COR)
  • Certificate of Airworthiness (COA)
  • Aircraft Insurance Certificate
  • Noise Certificate
  • Radio Station License
  • Aircraft specifications and performance data

🏢 Operator Documents

  • Air Operator Certificate (AOC)
  • Operating License or equivalent
  • Operator liability insurance
  • Company registration documents
  • Crew licenses and medical certificates
  • Flight plan and route details

📋 Additional Notes

Some countries may require additional documentation such as passenger manifests, cargo manifests, dangerous goods declarations, or diplomatic notes. Our permits team will advise you on all specific requirements for your route.
